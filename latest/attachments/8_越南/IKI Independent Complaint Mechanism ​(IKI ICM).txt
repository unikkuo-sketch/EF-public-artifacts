                        <div class="c-page-title u-space-top:default"><h1 class="u-typo:xxxl u-typo:bold2">How to file a complaint</h1></div>
                        


    <div class="o-grid__row" id="c8640">
        
        <div class="o-grid__col:12 o-grid__col:8@md">
            
                
                    


    <div id="c4327" class="c-text u-color-bg-width:default u-space-top:default">
        
        
            

    



        
        <p>The IKI Independent Complaint Mechanism (ICM) works worldwide to help resolve issues concerning IKI projects.</p><ul class="c-list c-list--unordered"> 	<li>Any person or a group of persons, or a community that has been or may be affected negatively by an IKI project may file a complaint.</li> 	<li>Also, persons wishing to do so can report integrity and/or corruption issues, such as misuse of funds, fraud etc.</li> 	<li>Last but not least,&nbsp;, if persons experience reprisals in relation to an IKI project or a complaint, they can also use this channel.&nbsp;The affected person(s) can authorise a representative to file and pursue the complaint on their behalf.</li> </ul><p>A complaint can be filed in English, or in the local language of the complainant. Where possible, a translation should be provided in English. Otherwise, the IKI ICM will have the complaint translated and respond in the language of the complainant.</p>
<p>The IKI ICM will provide confidentiality upon receiving a complaint if requested to do so by the complainant. This includes the names and identities of complainants and any designated representatives.
